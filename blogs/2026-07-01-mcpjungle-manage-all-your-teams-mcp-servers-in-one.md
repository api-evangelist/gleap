MCPJungle is an open-source gateway that centralizes Model Context Protocol servers, letting teams register each server once and expose them all through a single endpoint instead of managing multiple local installations. The article explains how it integrates with Gleap Kai to give AI agents unified, access-controlled access to internal systems.
